# Pakistan-Ecommerce-Dashboard
Excel dashboards analyzing Pakistan e-commerce sales dataset (2016–2018)
The purpose of this project is to explore Pakistan’s emerging e-commerce market, identify trends, and visualize actionable insights for decision-making.

---

## Dataset
- **Source:** Collected from various e-commerce merchants in Pakistan  
- **Time period:** March 2016 – August 2018  
- **Size:** 101 MB (CSV file)  
- **Unit of Analysis:** E-Commerce Orders  
- **Variables:** 
  - Item ID  
  - Order Status (Completed, Cancelled, Refund)  
  - Date of Order  
  - SKU  
  - Price  
  - Quantity  
  - Grand Total  
  - Category  
  - Payment Method  
  - Customer ID  

---

## Dashboards
1. **Monthly Sales Analysis:** Line chart showing total revenue per month  
2. **Category Revenue & Units Sold:** Bar chart comparing revenue and units across categories  
3. **Orders Analysis:** Pie chart of order status distribution  
4. **Payment Method Analysis:** Pie chart of orders by payment method  

---

## Tools Used
- **Excel**: Pivot Tables, Charts, Conditional Formatting

## Key Insights
- Highest sales observed in May (example: peak revenue month)  
- **Mobile & Tablets** category generates the most revenue  
- **Cash on Delivery (COD)** is the most preferred payment method  
- Majority of orders are completed  

---

## How to Use
1. Download the Excel file from `Excel_Dashboard/Ecommerce_Dashboard.xlsx`  
2. Explore pivot tables to filter data by month, category, or payment method  
3. Use dashboards to visualize trends, identify patterns, and generate insights  

---

## Inspiration
This project aims to inspire analysis on:
- Correlation between payment method and order status  
- Correlation between order date and product category  
- Hidden patterns in e-commerce trends  
- Predicting future orders, revenue, or popular product categories
